# SAP FusionForge

## Mission Statement

A SAP BTP Center of Excellence that delivers enterprise-grade solutions by fusing pro-code engineering with low-code agility — so business and IT move as one team.

## What We Do

SAP FusionForge operates on a hub-and-spoke delivery model. Our Solution Architect serves as the central orchestrator, translating business vision into technical reality. They coordinate a team of specialists spanning the full SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) stack, ensuring architectural cohesion and governance across every engagement.

Specialist agents cover key domains: CAP development for core business services, SAP Build Apps for rapid application development, SAP Build Process Automation for workflow orchestration, and BTP administration for platform lifecycle management. Business Analysts ensure requirements are captured with precision. The Discovery Advisor runs in parallel with the Business Analyst to align every engagement with existing SAP Discovery Center missions — so the Solution Architect always knows what's available before designing a landscape.

The team serves any client industry via a pluggable `CLIENT.md` config file (default scenario: `hr-services`). Swap the file, change the industry — same agents, same skills, any context.

## Core Objective

To democratize SAP BTP delivery by enabling citizen developers to contribute safely and effectively alongside seasoned pro-code engineers. We lower the barrier to enterprise innovation by providing guardrails, governance, and collaboration frameworks that accelerate delivery while maintaining production-grade standards.
